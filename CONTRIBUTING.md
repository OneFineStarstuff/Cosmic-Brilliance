# Contributing to Cosmic Brilliance

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Cosmic Brilliance! We welcome research contributions that push the boundaries of AI and theoretical physics.

## Research Ethics

As this project involves AGI prototypes and advanced AI reasoning, we prioritize safety and ethics. All contributions must adhere to the following principles:
- **Transparency:** Clearly document methodology and theoretical foundations.
- **Safety:** Assess potential risks of autonomous systems.
- **Integrity:** Ensure all physics-informed models are mathematically sound.

## How to Contribute

1. **Discuss first:** For major research changes, please open an issue to discuss the theoretical framework.
2. **Coding Standards:**
   - Use type hints for Python code.
   - For Jupyter Notebooks, ensure cells are cleared or outputs are justified for research visibility.
   - Maintain compatibility with standard libraries (PyTorch, TensorFlow Quantum, Qiskit).
3. **Pull Requests:**
   - Reference any relevant issues.
   - Include verification scripts or updated notebooks demonstrating the change.
   - Ensure `CITATION.cff` is updated if you are a major contributor.

## Community Standards

Please refer to our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) for expectations regarding professional behavior.
